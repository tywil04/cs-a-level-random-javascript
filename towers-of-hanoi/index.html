Towers of Hanoi

The goal is to move all of the disks on the left to the
right in order using the fewest moves possible.

You can only move if:
  - The top-most disk on the tower you are placing on 
    must have a larger mass.
  - The tower you are placing on is empty.
  - The disk you are placing is from the top of 
	its respective tower.

You can drag disks onto towers.

To load a game, use the load game button and to solve
the puzzle use the solve button. When using the solve
button ensure the gameboard has been reset, otherwise
unexpected issues may occur.
